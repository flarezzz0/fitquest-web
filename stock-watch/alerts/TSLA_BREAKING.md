## Summary
Reports emerged on May 26 that Elon Musk has internally considered combining Tesla (TSLA) and SpaceX into a single entity. While no formal proposal has been made, the mere consideration of merging a ~$1.6T automotive/energy/AI company with the world's leading private space/Starlink operator would represent one of the largest and most complex corporate restructurings in history.

Additionally, **DZ Bank upgraded TSLA from Sell → Hold** with a $385 price target, the first positive rating change after a streak of downgrades. TSLA closed at $433.59 (+1.78%) on the day.
