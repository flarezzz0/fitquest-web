# 🚨 AAPL - Bank of America Raises Price Target to $380

**Date:** May 26, 2026
**Severity:** 🟡 Medium

---

## Headline
**BofA analyst Wamsi Mohan raises Apple price target to $380 from $330 (+15%), reiterates Buy — cites "agentic AI" as major long-term revenue driver**

## Summary
On May 26, 2026, Bank of America Securities analyst **Wamsi Mohan** made a significant upward revision to Apple's price target:
- **New target:** $380 (up from $330)
- **Rating:** Buy (reiterated)
- **Thesis:** "Agentic AI" could become a major long-term revenue driver for Apple
- The raise represents a ~15% increase in target, implying ~20%+ upside from current levels

### Context
This upgrade comes amid ongoing questions about Apple's pace in AI compared to peers. BofA's analysis suggests Apple's massive installed base and ecosystem could make it a primary beneficiary of the agentic AI shift, similar to how the iPhone became the dominant mobile platform.
