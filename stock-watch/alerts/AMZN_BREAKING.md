# AMZN - BREAKING ALERT

**Headline:** Amazon to Acquire Globalstar for $11.6 Billion, Takes Over Apple's 20% Stake

**Date:** Late May 2026

## Summary
Amazon is acquiring satellite communications company Globalstar (GSAT) for $11.6 billion. As part of the acquisition, Amazon will take over Apple's existing 20% stake in Globalstar. Amazon stated the investment will strengthen the 1.6/2.4 GHz MSS that millions of iPhone users depend on for emergency satellite connectivity in areas without cellular coverage.

## Sources
- **TipRanks/The Fly:** Amazon to take over Apple's 20% stake in Globalstar as part of acquisition
- **Amazon IR:** Confirming Q1 FY26 net sales up 17% to $181.5B
